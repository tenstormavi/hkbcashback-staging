What is encashmore.com?
 Encashmore.com is a cashback website which gives you rewards in form of cash, for shopping through it on various partner websites like Amazon, Yatra, Paytm, Justeat and many more. There are no conditions attached. Just Shop and Earn Cash!
Which retailers can I save on?
 There are large number of retailers on which you can save through us. The list and the offers are updated regularly and hence you can check the same on the Homepage. In case you don’t find the retailer you are looking for, please drop us a mail with the details.
Do I need to pay to use encashmore.com?
 No, registration and use of the website is FREE OF COST.
How do I contact your Customer Support team?
 For all your queries and feedback you can mail to support@encashmore.com. We will reach back to you as soon as possible
How can I earn cashback via encashmore.com?
 You can earn the Cashback using the following simple steps.

  • Login to your account on Encashmore.com
  • Choose your category from amongst dining, shopping, fashion, travel, accessories & others
  • Click on the desired vendor from that category
  • You will be redirected to the vendor website. Shop Normally. And we will take care of the rest

Can I use vouchers along with cashback?
 Yes, you can use any coupons, vouchers and codes once you are on the merchant website. The cashback & rewards are over and above the discounts you would get and hence it is payable on the final bill amount.
What is the Process of the Cashback and how much time does it take?
 Once you have made a transaction, it usually takes 6-8 weeks for the complete process and for us to pay you. During the process you can see the following status on transaction page:

  • Tracked: Your transaction is recorded.
  • Approved: Once the transaction is tracked, the same is approved by the vendor. It usually takes 4-6 weeks.
  • Confirmed: We have received the payment from the vendor and shall be credited to you’re A/C as and when instructed by you. Usually takes 15 days after the transaction is approved.
  • Rejected:  In the rare case when your transaction is rejected by the Retailer in spite of our following up with him. The status against that transaction shall be shown as Rejected.
From the time I do a transaction, how long does it take to actually get my cashback?
 From the time of your transaction, it can take up to 8 weeks for the cashback. However it usually takes much lesser time.
Where do I see my cashback earnings?
 To view your earnings, Login in to your account, Click on ‘OPTIONS’ & then click on ‘My Transactions’
The retailer link is not working. What can I do?
 Please let us know on support@encashmore.com. We will get back to you as soon as possible
What are some best practices when making a purchase through encashmore.com?
 Please take care of the following before making the transaction
  • Do not add products to your cart before going to the vendor website through Encashmore.
  • Close all other tabs before visiting Encashmore.com and do not open new tabs unless the transaction has been completed.
  • Do not use Adblocker or other such software. Also, if possible clear all your cookies before proceeding.
  • Do not open other coupon sites during your purchase.
What information do I need to submit for a cashback ticket?
 You can open a missing cashback ticket by logging in  to your account & Clicking on ‘OPTIONS’ followed by  ‘ ADD MISSING TRANSACTION’ , you can log a ticket 72 hours after your transaction.
What are the reasons for a cashback to ‘not track’ or declined by the retailer?
 The transaction might have been declined because of the following reasons: 
  • The purchase was returned, cancelled or the order amended.
  • The purchase was not completed wholly online.
  • Another referrer has been awarded the cashback commission (e.g. a price comparison site).
  • You did not comply with the terms and conditions listed on our website and/or the retailer's.
  • A voucher code or gift code not authorized by our website was used.
  • The retailer has ceased trading or has gone into administration.
  • Cookies were disabled and hence the tracking could not be completed.
  • Ad blocker software was active on your machine
  • A coupon website or a price-comparison website was visited before completion of the transaction.

Generally, sticking to the terms and conditions provided on our retailer page and ensuring you follow the recommended process will minimize the risk of anything going wrong.

How do I request payment of my cashback?
 You can request for cashback when you have at least Rs. 50 in your earnings. You can send an email with your bank details to payments@encashmore.com to get your cashback. Or you can click on the ‘REDEEM’ button and we shall get in touch with you. You’re A/C will be credited with the amount in 8-10 working days.
